About the film

Stillness, nature,
and escape.

A brand film for Rogue Watches — an Australian independent watch brand built on the idea that a timepiece should be as intentional as the life it measures. The brief was simple: show the watch the way it deserves to be seen.

Shot with a focus on texture, light, and the quiet details that define fine craftsmanship — this film was made to feel more like a short film than a commercial. Because that's what the product deserved.

The approach

Documentary-level craft applied to commercial work. Every frame considered. Every transition earned. The goal was to make something that didn't feel like advertising — something people would actually want to watch.
