Who I am

Meaning over
everything.
Always.

I'm a documentary filmmaker and creative director based in Byron Bay. I've spent my career chasing the stories that actually matter — the ones that reveal something true about what it means to be alive.

I believe the best work comes from people who are fully themselves. That every frame is a choice. That craft without meaning is just noise. And that a camera, in the right hands, can change how someone sees the world.

My films have screened at international festivals. I've worked as Creative Director for some of the world's most-watched creators. I've built an audience of tens of thousands through craft alone. And I've spent years figuring out how to build a creative life that funds itself — without compromising the work that matters.

Featured Film — 2025

Flowing
Wood.

"Painting country, on country, with country."

A solitary traveler, surfer, and artist roams Australia's remote coastlines, turning weathered wood into stunning aerial seascapes. Gathering materials from the very beaches he visits, he finds solace in the rhythm of the ocean and the creative process.

Through art, he navigates themes of nature, solitude, and healing — transforming loss into something beautiful, one brushstroke at a time.
