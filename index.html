Who Am I

Meaning over
everything.
Always.

I'm a documentary filmmaker and creative director based in Byron Bay. I've spent my career chasing the stories that actually matter. The ones that reveal something true about what it means to be alive, and our shared connection to each other and this earth.

I believe the best work comes from people who are fully themselves. That every frame is a choice. That craft without meaning is just noise. And that a camera, in the right hands, can bring more human connection into the world.

I picked up a camera at 14 and never put it down. Since then it's taken me across Australia, through Indonesia, Kenya, and beyond, documenting the people and stories that remind us what life is really about. My films have screened at international festivals. I've worked as Creative Director for some of the world's most-watched creators. And I've spent years building a creative life that funds itself... without compromising the work that matters.

"My camera saved my life and gave me purpose, now I use it to tell stories that matter."
